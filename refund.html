Refund Policy

This policy explains when refunds may be offered for AI Market services.

1. Digital Nature of Services

AI Market provides digital content and services (videos, images, audio, etc.). Once work has started, costs such as AI processing and manual editing are involved.

2. Before Work Starts

If you have paid and request cancellation before we begin working on your order, we may provide a partial or full refund, depending on the situation.

3. After Work Has Started

Once we start working on your project, full refunds are generally not possible. We will try to resolve issues via revisions or adjustments where reasonable.

4. Quality Issues

If you are unhappy with the result, please contact us. In many cases we can offer:

  • Small corrections or minor edits.
  • Partial rework, if agreed.
  • Partial refund in special situations, at our discretion.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after the project is completed as per given instructions.
  • Delays caused by late responses, missing files or unclear instructions.
  • Platform issues (e.g., social media copyright claims) outside our control.

6. How to Request a Refund

Contact us via WhatsApp with your payment details, order details and reason for the request. We will review and respond as soon as possible.

This is a general refund policy for digital services. Your consumer rights may vary based on your country.
