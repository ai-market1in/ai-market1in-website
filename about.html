About AI Market

AI Market is a creative AI service platform that turns your ideas into videos, images and audio.

Who We Are

AI Market is built to help creators, small businesses and individuals use the power of AI without needing any complex software skills. You simply share your song, photos or idea, and we handle everything from editing to final export.

What We Do

  • Short video creation for Reels, Shorts and Status.
  • Cartoon and enhanced photos for social media.
  • Custom background music and vocal cleanup.
  • End-to-end support via WhatsApp, from concept to delivery.

Our Vision

We want AI Market to be a simple, trusted place where anyone in the world can get professional-looking content at a fair price, with human support and clear communication.

How We Work

  1. You place an order or contact us on WhatsApp.
  2. We confirm your idea, style and language requirements.
  3. Our AI + human workflow creates your content.
  4. We share preview, collect feedback and deliver final output.
