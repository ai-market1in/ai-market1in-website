Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Market handles information when you use our services.

1. Information We Receive

When you contact us or place an order, you may provide:

  • Name and contact details (e.g., WhatsApp number).
  • Content files such as audio, images, video and text.
  • Project requirements and preferences.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information mainly to:

  • Create and deliver your requested content.
  • Communicate about pricing, revisions and delivery.
  • Improve our services and internal workflows.

3. File Handling & Storage

Project files may be temporarily stored on secure devices or cloud services to complete your order. We do not intentionally resell or publish your personal content. However, you may separately give us permission to showcase certain outputs in our portfolio.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools such as payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay), cloud storage or messaging platforms (e.g., WhatsApp). These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We may keep project files and chat history for a limited period for support, reference or quality control. You can request deletion of your files where reasonably possible.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Please avoid sharing highly sensitive personal information in project files.

7. Children

Our services are not targeted at children under the age where parental consent is required in your region. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page or WhatsApp.
